Numeric and qualitative seizure frequency rules:

Baseline seizure frequency may be numeric or qualitative.

If a baseline seizure frequency contains a number:

"12 seizures per month"

Extract:

baseline_seizure_frequency_value = "12"
baseline_seizure_frequency_unit = "per_month"

If a baseline seizure frequency is qualitative:

"Prior to RNS implantation, she was experiencing daily seizures."

Extract:

baseline_seizure_frequency_value = "daily"
baseline_seizure_frequency_unit = "qualitative"

Other qualitative baseline values include:

- daily
- frequent
- occasional
- rare

Do not leave baseline seizure frequency blank if a numeric or qualitative baseline frequency is explicitly documented.

Do not mark an adverse event as absent unless the note explicitly denies that specific event or states it is not present.

If the note does not mention a specific adverse event, use not_listed.

Do not infer absence from silence.

Example:

If the note says "She denies tingling, painful stimulation, or sleep disruption from stimulation,"
then:
ae_stimulation_paresthesia_status = absent
ae_stimulation_discomfort_status = absent
ae_sleep_disruption_status = absent
ae_speech_disturbance_status = not_listed
